About Aimfinite Travel and Tour

We Are Jolito & Cheryl — and These Islands Are Our Home.

Aimfinite Travel and Tour was founded in 2017 by husband-and-wife team Jolito and Cheryl Comaingking, both born and raised in the Visayas. What started as a passion project — showing friends the hidden corners of their beloved islands — grew into one of Cebu's most trusted boutique travel agencies.

Eight years later, they have personally guided over 5,000 travelers from 32 countries through the whale sharks of Oslob, the Chocolate Hills of Bohol, the sardine run at Moalboal, and hundreds of moments in between. Every tour is still run with the same care they brought to their very first guest.
